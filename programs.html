Empowering Youth to End Malnutrition in Uganda

We are a national youth-led platform advocating for better nutrition and food security governance to create change in communities across Uganda.

Our Programs

Our programs encompass a wide range of activities recommended by health facilities and proven effective by specialists to enhance and improve a person's nutrition and health status, boosting living conditions for all. As a network, we have many inline programs to achieve with coordination from all beneficiaries and supporters.


Nutrition and HIV/AIDS

Food security and nutrition are fundamental to HIV treatment. We advocate for nutritional support to improve ART adherence and health outcomes for young people living with HIV.

Sustainable Agriculture

Promoting climate-smart agricultural practices to enhance food security and nutrition among youth, with special focus on empowering young women farmers.

Nutrition + SRHR

Ensuring access to SRHR for all, including adolescents and youth, by advocating for scaling up nutrition interventions and political commitment.

WASH & Nutrition

Implementing nutrition-sensitive water, sanitation, and hygiene interventions to address both acute and chronic food insecurity in vulnerable populations.

Nutrition Education

Creating awareness of healthy eating lifestyles, promoting food diversity, and educating youth on the importance of nutrition in physical activities and overall health.

Policy Advocacy

Advocating for improved financial, policy and legal action on nutrition, and holding stakeholders accountable for commitments to end malnutrition by 2030.

Nutrition and governance

Nutrition governance refers to the policies, institutions, and processes that ensure food and nutrition security within a society. Effective nutrition governance is crucial for integrating nutrition into development priorities, fostering collaboration across sectors, and ensuring accountability and resource allocation for addressing malnutrition and building resilience, especially in low- and middle-income countries

Nutrition and Climate change

Climate change significantly impacts nutrition by affecting food security and the nutritional value of food, leading to various forms of malnutrition. It also exacerbates existing health issues related to nutrition and strains healthcare systems

Nutrition and Gender Equality

Gender equality and good nutrition are intrinsically linked; gender inequality can both cause and be exacerbated by malnutrition. Addressing gender inequalities is crucial for achieving optimal nutrition for all, particularly for women and girls who are disproportionately affected by malnutrition

Nutrition and TB

Nutrition plays a crucial role in both the prevention and treatment of tuberculosis (TB). Undernutrition increases the risk of developing TB, and TB can, in turn, worsen nutritional status. Addressing nutritional needs is therefore essential for managing TB effectively

Nutrition and Humanitarian Response

Nutrition plays a vital role in humanitarian response, encompassing the assessment, prevention, and treatment of malnutrition in emergency situations. The goal is to ensure affected populations have access to adequate, safe, and nutritious food and essential nutrition services, while also addressing underlying factors that contribute to malnutrition

Nutrition and Trade

Trade can significantly impact nutrition, both positively and negatively, through various channels. It can increase food availability and affordability, diversify diets, and improve access to nutritious foods. However, it can also lead to an increased availability of less healthy foods, including ultra-processed options, potentially harming dietary quality

Nutrition and Communication

Nutrition and communication are closely linked, as effective communication is crucial for promoting healthy eating habits and addressing nutritional challenges. Nutrition communication encompasses various strategies and channels to disseminate information about nutrition, impacting individuals and communities.

About SUNFOYAC-CSN

Scaling Up Nutrition and Food Security Youth, Children and Adolescent Civil Society Network (SUNFOYAC-CSN) Uganda is a national youth-led platform advocating for better nutrition and food security governance.
